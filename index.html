WikiLorean

Browse historical data from Wikipedia. Note that the news tab is fetched from Current events, which could differ from Wikipedia's homepage news section. If checkbox "old links" is filled, articles link to their versions as they were the day of given search date, but request may take significantly longer. Insert "r" instead of day, month and year (or one/two of listed) to randomize the search.
